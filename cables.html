SHIELDED CABLES

A shielded cable or screened cable is an electrical cable of one or more insulated conductors enclosed by a common conduc- tive layer. The shield may be composed of braided strands of copper (or other metal, such as aluminium), a non-braided spiral winding of copper tape, or a layer of conducting polymer.
